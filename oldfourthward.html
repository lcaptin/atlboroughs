Old Fourth Ward

Old Atlanta's New Scene

Go

The historic home of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., the Old Fourth Ward emerged as one of the first middle class African American neighborhoods in the country.
Today it is a diverse neighborhood of historic sites, beautiful parks, culinary excellence, and exciting night life.
